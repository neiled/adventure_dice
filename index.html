Adventure Dice

Digitize your dice

Adventure Dice allows you to roll 18 different types of dice, set modifiers on them and even save your most used dice combinations to re-roll them at the touch of a button.

Coming soon: Combined with the iPad app all players can see all rolls that are made during the game (except for the secret DM rolls of course!)

Download today

Fun

Adventure Dice helps keep the game flowing whilst still maintaining the thrill of landing that d20.

Easy

Just pick your dice and roll away, or pick from your 'favorite' list to roll a pre-determined group of dice.

Modifier Support

Need to be able to roll a d20+3? No problem, full modifier support built in.

iPad Support [Coming Soon]

Put an iPad in the middle of the gaming area and now all the dice rolls that are made are shown on the iPad too!
